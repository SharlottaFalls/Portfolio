MY HOBBIES

Here are some things I like doing in my free time

My hobbies include...

Playing Violin. I started playing violin when I was 7 and have been playing ever since, I like it because music lets me express myself better than I could ever do with words. Drawing. I can't draw very well, but despite that doodling makes me relax during stressful situations and when I have time for a full drawing I can fully immerse myself in it and not think about anything else. Horse Riding. I'm not sure when my love for horses started, but it seems like since brth because I can't live without horses and I absolutely love the equestrian sport. Crochet. This is someting I picked up one time when I was bored during winter break and thought to myself that I might as well crochet some hats and blankets to stay warm while I'm at it, and I like to think that I am fairly good at it, plus I still use the clothes that I made during the cold whether and it's very comfy and stylish. Reading. Reading just helps me calm down and I absolutely love fantasizing about the world I'm reading about and the characters in it.
